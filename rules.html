Cards and their values

  • The cards that have a number on them have the same value as the number(e.g 2=2, 8=8 etc.).
  • J, Q, K share the same value: 10
  • A can have the value 1 or 11, depending on the other cards you have(e.g if you have a 2, A has the value 11, if you have a 10 and 2, A is 1).

Gameplay

The game is played with multiple sets of cards.

The dealer and the player(s) both start with 2 cards. One of the dealers' cards is not shown to the player(s).
The goal is to be as close to 21 as possible without going above it.

The dealer has to get another card if the value of their cards does not reach 17.

  • If a player goes above 21, they automatically lose whether the dealer goes above 21 or not.
  • If a player has 2 cards and their value is 21 (10+11), they automatically win. The same applies to the dealer.
  • If the player and the dealer share the same value of cards, it's a draw.
  • If a player has 5 cards and doesn't go above 21, they automatically win. Same applies to the dealer.
